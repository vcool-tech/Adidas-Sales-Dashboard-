## Project Summary

This project involved creating a comprehensive, one-page sales dashboard in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze Adidas US sales data. The goal was to visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) and identify regional and product trends.

## Key Insights & Metrics

* **Total Performance:** Sales were **$900M** and Profit was **$332M**.
* **Sales Mode:** The majority of sales and profit were generated **In-store** (highest sales from In-store: $666,643,750).
* **Top Products:** The top-selling product category is **Men's Street Footwear**.
* **Regional Performance:** Visual comparison of sales across Midwest, Northeast, South, Southeast, and West regions.

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated

* **Tools:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced formulas, Pivot Tables, Charts, and Conditional Formatting).
* **Skills:** Data Visualization, Sales Analysis, Business Intelligence Dashboard Design.
